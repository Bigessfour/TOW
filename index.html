About Wiley

The Town of Wiley is a vibrant rural community where agriculture and family values thrive. Our motto, "Down Wiley Way, It's Kids and Hay," reflects our commitment to supporting both our agricultural heritage and the families who call Wiley home.

We provide essential government services while maintaining the small-town charm that makes our community special. From youth programs to agricultural support, we're here to serve all residents with accessibility and inclusion as our guiding principles.
