Frequently Asked Questions

You can pay your utility bill online through our secure payment portal. Visit the "Pay Bills" section under Quick Links, create an account, and follow the instructions. You'll need your account number and ZIP code.

Town council meetings are held the second Tuesday of each month at 7:00 PM in the Town Hall council chambers. Public comment is welcome during the designated time on the agenda.

Building permit applications can be submitted online through our digital forms system or in person at Town Hall. Required documents include site plans, construction drawings, and proof of insurance.

Trash pickup is every Tuesday, and recycling pickup is every other Friday. Place bins at the curb by 7:00 AM. Check our community calendar for holiday schedule changes.

Report street issues through our online service request form under Quick Links, call Public Works at (555) 123‑4571, or use the feedback form on this website. Include the location and description of the problem.

Privacy Policy & Security

Information Collection & Use

The Town of Wiley is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or use our online services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, address, email, phone number when you submit forms or create accounts
  • Payment Information: Billing details for online payments (processed securely through encrypted third-party services)
  • Usage Data: Website analytics, page views, and interaction patterns (anonymized)
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information for security and functionality

How We Use Information

  • Providing government services and processing requests
  • Communicating about town services, meetings, and announcements
  • Processing payments and maintaining account records
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations and public records laws

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. Information may be shared:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • For public records requests (as permitted by state law)
  • With service providers who assist in website operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • In emergency situations to protect public safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers with access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Staff training on data protection

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we maintain
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request deletion of information (subject to legal retention requirements)

Cookies & Tracking

This website uses cookies to improve functionality and user experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser. Essential cookies for security and functionality cannot be disabled.
